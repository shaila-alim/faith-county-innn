Just Come To Us & Relax

FAITH County Inn has started with a dream to create a best place for active people. Initiated by the prestegious FAITH Group, the resort has so many activities to do, be it your Family Functions or a Corporate Event, it is a unique experience that you and your family will never forget. Weddings at FAITH County Inn are memorable for years. We at County Inn want to provide the best active holiday experience in the country, built around sport, a healthy and active lifestyle and social activities.

Located in the heart of India. At the capital city Bhopal of Madhya Pradesh, FAITH County Inn alongwith FCC spreaded in arround 16 acres of lush green surroundings. Bhopal is well connected from all major cities of India. A developing city with clear air, sun and warmth all year round.
